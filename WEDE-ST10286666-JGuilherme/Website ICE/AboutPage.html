What are we about?

Our program/Cause is here to help spread there word and strive forward to help the Amazon Rainforest
and all living organisims that reside within that is why even a simple donation can help make a diffrence.
By helping us you are saving many species and indigenous people from starvation and a lack of shelter and
saving many trees from deforestation. We want to help engage the world in many ways of saving the Amazon
Rainforest. We plan to do this by getting people to help by Donations, Registering for our Outreach program
where they can help replant trees and help rebuild infrastructure of the indigenous peoples homes as well as
help them rebuild farms and there crop. This will help maintain the population from decreasing as well as the
Animals population becoming maintained. We also plan on taking species on the risk of extinction and taking them
to a rehabilation centre to keep them until there habitat can be restored so then we can keep watch of them and
feed them until they are ready to go back to the Rainforest. Here is a quick summary of what you can do to help.

What can i do to help?
→ Donations of any kind Examples being Money, Food and water.
→ Registering online for volunteering to plant trees or help out the indigenous people.
→ Recycling of any sort and try to reduce the amount of paper usage in your household, school or business.
→ You can tell your friends and family about the cause to spread the word has there is power in numbers.
